Welcome to MugLife Coffee, your neighborhood coffee shop located in the heart of Kalpetta, Wayanad, Kerala. At MugLife, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience, a moment of joy, and a way to connect with others.

Our Story
Founded in 2025, MugLife started as a cozy local café with one mission: to share the love of quality, ethically-sourced coffee with our community.

Our passion for travel and coffee led us on a journey across the coffee-growing regions of India, especially the lush estates of Wayanad and Coorg, where we handpicked partnerships with small farms and cooperatives. We ensure that every cup we brew tells a story of dedication and care, from farm to table. Our beans are roasted in-house to bring out the unique flavors that reflect the richness of Kerala’s coffee heritage.

Delivery & Locations Served
In addition to offering a warm and welcoming space to enjoy coffee in our café, we proudly deliver to Kalpetta and nearby areas in Wayanad. Whether you’re at home, at work, or relaxing in the scenic hills, we bring your favorite coffee right to your door. Our delivery service ensures you never miss your daily cup, no matter where you are in Wayanad.

Our Menu
Our menu offers something for everyone—from our signature espresso blends to refreshing cold brews, artisanal teas, and fresh-baked goods from local bakeries. We also cater to a variety of dietary needs with a range of plant-based milk options and gluten-free snacks.

Community & Sustainability
At MugLife, we are more than just coffee. We are part of the Wayanad community, and we care deeply about sustainability. We use eco-friendly packaging, work with local farmers, and strive to minimize our carbon footprint. Our café regularly hosts events such as live music nights, art showcases, and community meetups, making it a hub for creativity and connection.
